V0 - MVP
--------

* 30 second set up
* responsive - manage your entire site, from your phone
* fully compressed content sent with caching headers
* very simple WYSIWYG editor, quick and easy image add
* images
* menu designer (that's easy)
* apache support
* nginx support
* URL agnostic; works on http://local:8080/ the same as http://www.website.com
